Welcome to Whiteboard!

Whiteboard is an American educational technology company with its corporate headquarters in Boston Massachusetts. It is best known for Whiteboard Learn, a learning management system. Designed at Northeastern University, Whiteboard is one of the most prevalent educational tools for online learning which gained a great deal of traction during the 2020 COVID-19 outbreak.

Whiteboard Features:

Fastest Growing online platform!
Whiteboard grew by 150% in the year 2020 driven by strong sales and high demand.
Most Advanced Educational Tool!
Whiteboard was rated the nations top education online tool because of its advanced capabilities.
Safest for Online Learning
In 2020, Whiteboard was rated the safest environment for online learning by the department of education.
Most Affordable tool
As one of the largest platforms, Whiteboard was rated as the most affordable tool for online education.
Effective Learning
Whiteboard was noted to be the most effective tool for online learning, even for students that prefer in-person learning..
Easy to Learn
Whiteboard was rated as one of the best user-friendly platforms when it comes to online education tools in 2020.
